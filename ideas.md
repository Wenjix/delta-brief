<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Swiss Style / International Typographic Style** (Modernized)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Clarity above all**: The content (brief) is the hero; UI should recede.
  2. **Grid-based precision**: Everything aligns perfectly; structured information hierarchy.
  3. **Objective presentation**: Unbiased, factual, "executive" feel.
  4. **High contrast**: Bold distinctions between sections and data points.

  **Color Philosophy**: 
  - **Monochrome base**: Black, white, and shades of cool gray (#F5F5F7, #E5E5EA).
  - **Signal Red (#FF3B30)**: Used sparingly for "Risks" and critical alerts.
  - **Focus Blue (#007AFF)**: For primary actions and "Moves".
  - *Reasoning*: Evokes the feeling of a printed executive report or a high-end financial terminal. Serious, urgent, and professional.

  **Layout Paradigm**: 
  - **Split-screen / Asymmetric**: On desktop, navigation/context on the left (narrow), content on the right (wide).
  - **Card-less**: Content sits directly on the background or within subtle dividers rather than floating cards.
  - **Sticky headers**: Section titles stick as you scroll, reinforcing structure.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **Heavy Dividers**: Thick black (or dark gray) lines separating major sections.
  2. **Monospace Data**: Dates, IDs, and metrics in a crisp monospace font.
  3. **"Redacted" Loading State**: Loading skeletons look like redacted text, hinting at "classified" intel.

  **Interaction Philosophy**: 
  - **Instant & Snap**: Transitions are fast (0.1s), almost instant. No bouncy springs.
  - **Hover-to-reveal**: Secondary details (metadata) appear on hover to keep the initial view clean.

  **Animation**: 
  - **Slide & Fade**: Content slides in from the bottom with a quick fade.
  - **Typewriter effect**: For the brief generation, text appears character-by-character (fast) to simulate real-time intelligence gathering.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Helvetica Now Display** (or Inter Tight), Heavy weight, tight tracking.
  - **Body**: **Inter**, Regular weight, high legibility.
  - **Data/Labels**: **JetBrains Mono** or **Roboto Mono**, uppercase, small size.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Brutalist / Neo-Brutalist** (Softened for Enterprise)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw & Unfiltered**: Show the "guts" of the data; don't hide complexity, organize it.
  2. **Bold Borders**: Everything has a defined edge.
  3. **System fonts**: Use the native OS font stack to feel "native" and fast.
  4. **High utility**: Density is a feature, not a bug.

  **Color Philosophy**: 
  - **Paper White (#FFFFFF)** & **Ink Black (#000000)**.
  - **Highlighter Yellow (#FAFF00)**: For "New" or "Changed" items.
  - **Safety Orange (#FF5F00)**: For warnings.
  - *Reasoning*: Mimics a physical dossier or a marked-up document. Feels like a tool, not a toy.

  **Layout Paradigm**: 
  - **Dashboard Grid**: Modular blocks that fit together like masonry.
  - **Visible Grid Lines**: Subtle lines separating every component.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **Hard Shadows**: Buttons and cards have solid, non-blurred shadows (4px offset).
  2. **Marquee**: Scrolling text for "Breaking News" or updates (used sparingly).
  3. **Tag Clouds**: Dense clusters of keywords for "Memory Highlights".

  **Interaction Philosophy**: 
  - **Tactile**: Buttons depress visibly (transform: translate) on click.
  - **Direct Manipulation**: Drag-and-drop where possible (e.g., reordering moves).

  **Animation**: 
  - **None / Cut**: Changes happen instantly.
  - **Blink**: Cursors or indicators blink to show activity.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Space Grotesk** or **Archivo**, bold, slightly extended.
  - **Body**: **System UI** (San Francisco / Segoe UI).
  - **Accents**: **Courier New** for "raw data" look.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: **Glassmorphism / Ethereal Tech** (Dark Mode First)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Depth & Layering**: Information floats on different planes.
  2. **Glow & Light**: Important elements emit light.
  3. **Fluidity**: Everything flows into the next state.
  4. **Immersive**: The user enters a "focus mode".

  **Color Philosophy**: 
  - **Deep Space (#050510)**: Background.
  - **Neon Cyan (#00F0FF)**: Primary accents / AI generation.
  - **Electric Purple (#BD00FF)**: Secondary accents / Memory.
  - *Reasoning*: Feels futuristic, AI-native, and "magical".

  **Layout Paradigm**: 
  - **Centered Focus**: The main content (brief) is center-stage, surrounded by "floating" controls.
  - **Blurred Backdrops**: Panels are semi-transparent, showing hints of what's behind.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **Gradient Borders**: Thin, glowing borders on active cards.
  2. **Mesh Gradients**: Subtle, moving background blobs.
  3. **Glass Cards**: Frosted glass effect for all containers.

  **Interaction Philosophy**: 
  - **Fluid**: Elements morph into each other.
  - **Parallax**: Mouse movement shifts the background slightly.

  **Animation**: 
  - **Glow Pulse**: AI elements pulse while "thinking".
  - **Staggered Reveal**: Items float up one by one.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: **Outfit** or **Manrope**, geometric sans.
  - **Body**: **DM Sans**.
  - **Tech**: **Share Tech Mono** for decorative elements.
</idea>
